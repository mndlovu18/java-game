LET'S LEARN JAVA WHILE HAVING FUN!

This is going to be a two player game. The game will be divided into levels and when each level begins players are educated on a specific topic in Java then do a quiz. The player who gets most questions correct wins the round. When the first round is over you can move on to the second round or replay the initial roun. Questions and answers for a level will be made available to the players once that level has been completed. You also have the choice to make it a single palyer game.
